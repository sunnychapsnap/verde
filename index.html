    Inspired by nature, and committed to using seasonal, locally-grown flowers and herbs when possible, Verde Natural Florals can help you create a beautiful, affordable, and memorable event. Whether you are planning a simple backyard wedding or an elegant wedding at a local vineyard, let Verde Natural Florals design custom floral arrangements that are fresh, elegant and reasonably priced. We balance style with sustainability, ensuring your event is both gorgeous and socially responsible.

    Philosophy

    Recognizing the demand for affordable, socially-responsible flowers, Verde Natural Florals creates weddings and events that are beautiful and eco-friendly, yet reasonably priced and customized to fit your budget.

    With a horticultural background and a longtime passion for flowers and gardening, I specialize in designs that are garden-inspired and that use locally-grown, seasonal flowers, herbs and foliage whenever possible.

    I will work closely with you to bring your ideas and personality into your event, mindfully crafting each arrangement and bouquet in a unique and sustainable way. I will help guide your ideas to fruition, ensuring the flowers are a true reflection of you and your special event.

    Verde Natural Florals believes that fresh, natural and organic flowers do not have to be out of your price range. We can create a wedding or event that is simple, elegant and original, while remaining respectful of both mother earth and your budget.
